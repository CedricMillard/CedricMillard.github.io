Welcome to an online version of the escape game Unlock! in order to play remotely with friends or colleagues. How to play:
- Select a game below
- Install and run the Unlock App on your phone (links at the bottom of this page)
- Install and run TeamViewer QuickSupport on your mobile phone to share the screen with your computer
- Run TeamViewer on your PC and connect to your phone to display the phone screen
- With Skype, Teams or Zoom, share your computer screen with your friends showing both this website and the teamviewer screen

Enjoy !
